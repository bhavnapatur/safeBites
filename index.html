SafeBites
Our mission is to help people with dietary restrictions find safe recipes. This specific version is geared towards those who are diabetic or pre-diabetic to help manage daily sugar intake.
Why?
Diabetic patients lack a hormone called insulin, which regulates sugar levels. Because extremely high and low sugar levels can be dangerous, they have to manage their diet based on the recommended amount of free sugars they should be having. It can be difficult to find these specific recipes, so this website filters them.
